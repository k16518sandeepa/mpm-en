Black Mirror

Read this in Sinhala

Hey everyone, Today, we've brought you a series that is considered underrated in Sri Lanka: Black Mirror.

Black Mirror falls under the genres of Sci-Fi and Psychological Thriller, and it is an anthology series. This means that each episode tells a different story. However, if you look closely, it feels like a shared universe vibe, as every episode contains something that connects it to the others.

The main theme of Black Mirror explores potential events in the near future driven by technological advancements. It dives into how everyday tools like the internet, social media, gaming, virtual reality, and hacking evolve with technology, transforming human behavior and society. These themes echo across nearly every season.

The primary creator of Black Mirror is Charlie Brooker. It was originally broadcast on the UK channel Channel 4 before Netflix acquired the distribution rights.

In terms of ratings, it scores an impressive IMDB 8.7/10 and Rotten Tomatoes 83%, ranking 43rd on IMDB.

Finally, this is a must-watch series for anyone who uses technology. You should definitely check it out.
