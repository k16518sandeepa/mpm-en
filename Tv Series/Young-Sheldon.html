Young Sheldon(Beyond the Bazinga: A Look at the Heart of Young Sheldon).

Read this in Sinhala

Hello Guys,

Today, we're bringing you an article about an underrated series in Sri Lanka: Young Sheldon (Beyond the Bazinga: A Look at the Heart of Young Sheldon).

Regarding the story, even if you haven't watched it, if you're a fan of TV series, you've definitely heard of The Big Bang Theory. Young Sheldon is about the childhood life of Sheldon Cooper from The Big Bang Theory. Sheldon Cooper's childhood takes place primarily in the 1980s and 1990s. Sheldon is a child prodigy with a super brain (similar to Malcolm in the Middle from a series we uploaded previously). The series beautifully builds up how Sheldon's unique issues differ from other children's and how he interacts with his family, schoolmates, and others.

Iain Armitage plays the role of Sheldon. Zoe Perry portrays his mother, who supports Sheldon in all his endeavors. Lance Barber plays the father. Sheldon also has a sister and an older brother, and the way Sheldon interacts with them is presented wonderfully. Raegan Revord and Montana Jordan play the sister and brother, respectively. Furthermore, Annie Potts has a prominent role in the story as Sheldon's grandmother. Even Elon Musk, the CEO of companies like Space X and Tesla, has made an appearance.

Young Sheldon falls under the genres of Comedy and Sitcom. It was created by Chuck Lorre and Steven Molaro (Chuck Lorre also created The Big Bang Theory). Young Sheldon first premiered on the CBS Network on September 25, 2017. In terms of ratings, it has an IMDB score of 7.7/10 and a Rotten Tomatoes score of 76%. It ranks 165th on IMDB's popular TV show list. Young Sheldon has also won 8 awards and received 32 nominations.

So, Guys, to see how Sheldon navigates life with his family, friends, and society, go ahead and download and watch it! We'll meet again with another fantastic series soon.
