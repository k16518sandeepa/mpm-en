Nvidia Launches RTX 50 Series at CES 2025: Powered by Blackwell Architecture

Read this in Sinhala

Nvidia, the world's leading multinational technology company, officially unveiled its next-generation GPU lineup, the RTX 50 Series, during CES 2025.

The RTX 50 Series is powered by the NVIDIA Blackwell architecture (a new class of AI Superchip) and features significant AI enhancements, promising to fulfill gamers' dreams of massive performance boosts.

The lineup includes four GPUs: RTX 5090, RTX 5080, RTX 5070 Ti, and RTX 5070.

The new series introduces DLSS 4, an AI-powered technology that generates extra frames per second, ensuring an ultimate gaming experience during gameplay.

The RTX 50 Series is notable for being the first GPU generation to feature both GDDR7 Memory and PCIe Gen 5 technology. Additionally, the inclusion of DisplayPort 2.1b Support enables high resolutions and refresh rates for a superior viewing experience.
